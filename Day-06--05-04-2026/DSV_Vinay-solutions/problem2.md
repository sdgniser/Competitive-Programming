- 4 is the smallest composite number. Using as many 4s as possible gives the maximum value 
Below are the summation corresponding to maximum number of terms for any given number.
- 0 mod4 -> n//4
- 1 mod4 -> 4k + 1 = 4(k-2) + 9 -> n//4 -1
- 2 mod4 -> 4(k-1) + 6 -> n//4
- 3 mod4 -> 4(k-3) + 6 + 9 -> n//4 -1
