- First point to notice is that the minimum possible value of bn is n.
- If we deviate by any number in 1,2,3.. in sequence bi, we will end up having larger bn.
- The optimal way would be to just start assigning 1 to n and if any of the ai collide we increase our number by 1 and contiue. This gives us that the final bn will be collision numbner + n. So we just need to count the number of collisions.
